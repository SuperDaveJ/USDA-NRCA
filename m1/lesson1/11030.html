Why Do We Recruit?

Audio Transcript

NRCS’s mission, “Helping people help the land,” is centered on two things: conservation and people.  In order to meet this goal, the NRCS workforce must be comprised of highly-qualified, diverse people.

Recruiting is about making sure the agency has access to the right people with the right skills at the right time.  In order to meet the needs of the agency in a timely manner, it’s not enough to simply search for and find potential candidates.  There must be an established pipeline consistently moving candidates toward contributing skills to NRCS.

This reiterates that recruitment is more than hiring – it’s about creating and enhancing the image of the agency as a “Best Place to Work,” as well as getting information about the agency and its mission to the best and brightest future employees.
