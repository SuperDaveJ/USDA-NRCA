Welcome to the NRCS Recruitment Training course! 

You’re taking this course because you are qualified to become involved in formal recruiting efforts for NRCS.  The course will equip you to help NRCS accomplish its mission by providing information to potential candidates for employment, assuring that NRCS has access to the right people with the right skills at the right time.

This first lesson will introduce you to recruiting—what it is, and why it’s necessary at all.  You will also learn to differentiate between recruiting and hiring.

Along your journey of preparing to recruit for NRCS, you’ll have opportunities to hear from an expert recruiter.  Select the “From the Expert” icon now so she can introduce herself.

This lesson will take approximately 10 minutes to complete.

Select the From the Expert icon to learn more, and then FORWARD to continue.
