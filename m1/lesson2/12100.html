What Would the Expert Say?

Remember, an "elevator speech" is an opportunity to get your point across in a timely manner; namely, to define the organization, what’s been accomplished, and why it’s a “Best Place to Work.” Write your own elevator speech now so you have it ready when you need it for formal recruiting events or unplanned recruiting opportunities. When you're finished, you can compare the contents of your speech to an expert's.

Good afternoon, my name’s Samantha and I work with the Natural Resources Conservation Service, where we focus on protecting and enhancing natural resources such as soil quality, water quality and wildlife habitats. Our agency develops conservation plans and technological guides which help our customers with conservation efforts.

I enjoy working at NRCS in a relaxed environment, full of friendly people who have a love for actively taking a role to help the environment. It’s a great career, simply “Helping people help the land”, and I hope you will take some time to learn more about NRCS and consider employment with us if you have an interest in working in natural resources!

It was a pleasure meeting you! Take Care!
