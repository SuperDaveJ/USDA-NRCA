NRSC Employee

Hi, I’m a Soil Conservationist with the National Resource Conservation Service.  I spend my work days developing conservation plans.  Our agency focuses on many different efforts that preserve natural resources. I understand that what I do makes a difference on the land.  That’s what drives me - I like helping landowners reach their goals and see the positive results of the conservation work that is done.  We have offices that serve every county in all 50 states and also the US territories in the Pacific Basin and the Caribbean.  You can have a great career, simply “Helping People help the land”, and I hope you will take some time to learn more about NRCS.

It was a pleasure meeting you! Take Care!
