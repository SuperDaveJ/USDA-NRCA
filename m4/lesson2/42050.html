Long-term Care Insurance

The Federal Long-term Care Insurance program provides financial support to help with qualifying costs of assistance with daily living activities.  To be eligible for this program, the employee must be eligible for FEHB.  The long-term care benefit may apply to the employee or the employee’s spouse or dependents.

There are two options under this plan:  Facilities Only and the Comprehensive Plan.  Facilities Only covers assisted living outside the home.  The Comprehensive Plan covers assisted living outside the home and care provided at home.
