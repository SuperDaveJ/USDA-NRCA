Lesson Summary

As you have seen, there are significant benefits provided by the Federal government for NRCS employees.  Being familiar with these benefits allows you point out some of the many highlights of being an NRCS employee and to answer questions posed by potential applicants.

The information in this lesson is a general overview of these benefits so that you can help candidates make informed decisions about their employment choices.  For more detailed information, you should always refer candidates to a Human Resources representative.

Working for the NRCS provides competitive pay and many other Federal benefits.  You need to become familiar with each of the following:

  • Pay
  • Leave
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Long-term care insurance
  • Life insurance
  • Retirement plans
    • Basic benefit plan
    • Thrift savings plan
