Pay

The Federal government offers competitive pay and salary levels based on the General Schedule (GS) system.  The GS level for a given job is based on the nature of the position, level of education and academic achievement required for the position, and experience of the candidate.

Typically, pay for a given GS level is set at step one of the grade upon entry into the Federal government.  However, there could be variations to the pay if a candidate is eligible for application of the highest previous rate, meaning he or she has worked before and can receive pay at or above the previous rate being received.  Other situations may justify an advanced hiring rate based on certain superior qualifications of the candidate. Advanced hiring rates must be approved prior to the appointment.

Additionally, candidates could be eligible for appointment to a GS-7 level based on superior academic achievement, indicated by completion of a Bachelor’s degree, excellent class standing, superior grade point average, or election to membership in a national scholastic honor society.
