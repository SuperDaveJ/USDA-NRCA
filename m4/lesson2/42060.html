Life Insurance

Employees are eligible for group term life insurance coverage under the Federal Employees’ Group Life Insurance (FEGLI) Program.  In most cases, new Federal employees are automatically covered by Basic life insurance and the agency payroll office deducts premiums from the employee’s paycheck unless he or she waives the coverage. In addition to the Basic, there are three forms of Optional insurance.  You must have Basic insurance in order to elect any of the options. Unlike Basic, enrollment in Optional insurance is not automatic—the employee must take action to elect the options.

The cost of Basic insurance is shared between the employee and the Government; the employee pays 2/3 of the total cost and the Government pays 1/3. Age does not affect the cost of Basic insurance. However, the employee pays the full cost of Optional insurance, and the cost depends on your age.

This benefit does not build up any cash value or paid-up value.
