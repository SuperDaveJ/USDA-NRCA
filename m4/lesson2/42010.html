Lesson Overview

As we’ve discussed some already, a critical part of recruiting is promoting the advantages of working for NRCS.  While the ability to directly contribute to the conservation of the nation’s resources may be reward enough, there are tangible benefits for applicant’s to consider as well.  Your job as a recruiter is to identify which of these benefits are most important to potential applicants and to be able to discuss what NRCS and the Federal government have to offer.

The NRCS provides many benefits that allow its employees to successfully manage both life and career.  This lesson will help you understand these NRCS-specific benefits so you’re well-positioned to discuss them with potential applicants.

This lesson will take approximately 10 minutes to complete.
