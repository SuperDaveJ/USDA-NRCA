Employee Organizations

Audio Transcript

NRCS has many employee organizations to help its community grow and develop around commonalities and interests.  Here are just a few examples.

  • American Indian/Alaskan Native Association for NRCS
  • National Organization of Professional Black NRCS Employees
  • National Organization of Professional Hispanic NRCS Employees
  • OPEDA (Organization of Professional Employees), U.S. Department of Agriculture

For more information on the various employee organizations available to NRCS employees, visit:   http://www.nrcs.usda.gov/partners/employee_org.html
