What Would the Expert Say?

Imagine that you’re at a college job fair having a conversation with Margaret, a potential applicant.  She asks you to explain why she should consider working for NRCS above other Federal agencies.  What should you tell her?

At NRCS we offer challenging work that really pays off in impact on the environment. We’re well known for employing talented people, committed to the conservation and preservation of our country’s natural resources.

We have three regions which include all 50 states and territories in the Caribbean and Pacific. That means you could do real hands-on conservation planning in a variety of places, seeing the impact of your work in the community you serve.

To ensure a healthy balance between work and the rest of your life, we offer flexible work schedules and telework options.  We also believe in contributing to the professional development of our employees.  We offer many forms of training and a national mentoring program to enhance your career growth.
