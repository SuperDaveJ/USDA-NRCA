Lesson Summary

Now you can speak to potential applicants about the NRCS-specific benefits available to employees, such as flexible work schedules, continuing education, and involvement with employee organizations.

In the next lesson you will learn about the benefits that the Federal government provides for NRCS employees.

Work Life Balance

The NRCS provides many options that allow balance for work and personal life.  Among these are:

  • Flex schedules
  • Telework
  • Mobility Opportunities

Training and Development

To further professional development, the NRCS offers different resources that employees can use to their advantage, such as:

  • NRCS National Employee Development Center
  • AgLearn
  • National Mentoring Framework

Awards and Recognition

NRCS rewards great performance.  Employees may be eligible for two types of awards:

  • Performance Awards
  • Cash Awards

Employee Organizations

NRCS has many employee organizations to help its community grow and develop around commonalities and interests.

For more information on the various employee organizations available to NRCS employees, visit:   http://www.nrcs.usda.gov/partners/employee_org.html
