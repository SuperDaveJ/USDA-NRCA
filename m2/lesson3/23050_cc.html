Expert Recruiter

If you don’t know the answer to a candidate’s question, and are not sure where to refer them, offer them your business card so they can follow-up with you about their question after the event.  When doing so, explain to the candidate that you’d be happy to help them further if they will email or call you about their question in the next few days.  Tell them that, this way, you can be sure that their question will be in front of you when you have the resources available to address it.

Addressing candidate questions in this manner ensures that you don’t set a negative impression of the agency by promising to follow-up and then inadvertently forgetting.
