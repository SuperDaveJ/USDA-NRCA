The Importance of Follow-up

Whether you conduct interviews during a recruiting effort or not, when the event is over your job as a recruiter isn’t.  There are several important steps to complete in order to get the most out of the recruiting event or effort.

When you have completed your assessment, follow up with the official or department from whom you received the authorization to attend.

Make sure any resumes and interview information you collected are properly filed with Human Resources, and follow up with candidates as appropriate.

To assess the success and appropriateness of an event:

  • Were you able to reach and recruit the types of candidates you intended?
  • Was the event cost-effective?
  • Would you participate in this event or effort in the future?
