Talking with Candidates

The next step in building credibility is to talk with candidates in a knowledgeable, welcoming manner.  When you engage a potential applicant, you have to know how to orient them quickly to the mission of the agency and to find out what interests or motivates them.

Remember the elevator speech from Module 1, Lesson 2?  This is another chance to use it!

Other tips for building and maintaining credibility while talking with candidates include:

  • Ask discovery questions
  • Focus on the mission of NRCS specifically
  • Present information clearly and appropriately
  • Discuss positions and job titles knowledgably
