Fielding Application Questions

If you are asked very specific questions about the application process, you should refer applicants to the NRCS Human Resources department in your state.

You are encouraged to have a representative from HR on your team for recruiting events or efforts whenever possible.  If such a representative is not available, be sure to familiarize yourself with the application process for available positions as much as possible.

When answering questions about the application process, do not promise a position, or indicate whether someone is or is not qualified for a position.  Don’t promise a timeline for hiring, or when a potential applicant will receive a response about their application.

Make sure you know:

  • What the candidate should do to apply
  • What the position requirements are
  • What documents need to be included with the application
  • Contact information for who the candidate should speak to about questions
  • Where the candidate should send his or her application and required documents
