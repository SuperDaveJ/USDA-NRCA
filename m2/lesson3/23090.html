Informing Participants About Applying through USAJobs

Another important part of managing expectations while recruiting is being able to discuss the application process with potential candidates.

NRCS, like all Federal agencies, manages its application process through USAJobs.gov for many of its positions.  However, as you will learn in Module 3, some of the hiring authorities through which NRCS employs people allow for direct hiring, meaning an announcement does not have to be made through USAJobs.gov.  This distinction is location and/or position-dependent.

Detailed information on applying at USAJobs.gov can be found at: http://www.usajobs.gov/infocenter
