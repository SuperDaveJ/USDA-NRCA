Lesson Overview

The goal of recruiting is to ensure access to the right people with the right skills at the right time.  The first step in realizing this goal is to identify where and how the right people can be found.  You can then target your energy toward recruiting efforts and events that will increase your likelihood of finding the people with the desired qualities and skills. 

This lesson will help you identify what factors you should consider when trying to focus recruiting efforts and instruct you in selecting appropriate types of recruiting events and efforts based on particular agency needs.  You’ll also learn about the need discuss position qualifications and general requirements for working at NRCS with potential applicants.

This lesson will take approximately 25 minutes to complete.
