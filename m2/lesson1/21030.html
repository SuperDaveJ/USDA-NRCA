Finding Well-Qualified Applicants

The vision of the agency will not be realized without matching skills of its workforce to that vision.  Therefore, it’s important that potential candidates have acquired the technical expertise needed to perform their jobs with as little-needed supplemental training as possible.  In order to target these types of candidates, you need to identify appropriate sources.  A good strategy is to partner with schools, trade associations, or other organizations that draw candidates with particular skill sets and interests in conservation and agriculture.

Additionally, you must know what the qualifications are for each position within NRCS so that you can talk intelligently to potential applicants about their options within the agency.
