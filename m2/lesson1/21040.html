Qualifications for NRCS Positions

When recruiting, you should be able to speak intelligently about specific skills and education that NRCS requires of its employees at each position.  Select each photo below to hear about the qualifications needed for the jobs these NRCS employees perform.

For additional information on job requirements, visit: http://www.nrcs.usda.gov/about/challengingcareers.html.

Also, you can refer to the job aid of qualification cards found in the Resources section of this course in preparation for a recruiting event or to hand out to people during an event.  Keep in mind, there may be state-specific qualifications that need included in any qualification literature, so be sure to adjust the job aid as appropriate.
