Researching the Recruiting Market or Event

The first preparation step that you’ll complete is gaining an understanding of the recruiting event you plan to attend, and ensuring it fits with the needs of NRCS or your state specifically.  There are four steps involved in researching the event.

  • Clarify your needs
  • Identify possible events
  • Coordinate with event hosts to evaluate the audience
  • Analyze the event based on the cost and expected benefits
