Gathering or Ordering Recruiting Materials

Gather the recruiting materials you will need in advance of the effort or event.  You should consider including:

  • Brochures and flyers about NRCS and USAJobs.gov
  • Exhibits, props, or demonstration materials
  • Give-aways
  • Business cards
  • Nametags
  • Sign-in sheets and interview schedules
  • Pay chart
  • Office supplies, such as pens, pencils, paperclips, and staplers
  • Hand sanitizer

Think about the materials you bring in terms of the type of candidate you are looking to recruit, and the type of event you are attending.  For example, at colleges and universities, make sure to include materials describing internship opportunities or benefits including student loan repayment.  When attending diversity events, bring materials that promote opportunities and special emphasis programs.

You may need to order materials that you wish to take to the event such as banner stand exhibits and brochures.  Planning for what you’ll take in advance will allow time to place and receive the order you need.  To order materials such as exhibits or brochures, click on the Publications link on the NRCS home page to see what materials are available and instructions on how to order them.
