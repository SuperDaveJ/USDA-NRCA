Getting Approval and Selecting a Team

Audio Transcript

Once you have chosen an event to attend, or effort to focus on, there are several more preparation steps to complete before you head out.

First, you’ll need to get approval from your manager to move forward.  When you have approval, select the team you’ll bring with you to the event.  In addition to yourself and/or other recruitment representatives you should include two types of people on your team, a Human Resources (HR) representative and a field representative, if at all possible.

When assembling your team, you want to consider diverse representation.  Remember, diversity should be considered in a holistic way – as more than simply gender or race.

Finally, make arrangements to pay for the event if necessary.
