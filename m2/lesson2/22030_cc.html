Expert Recruiter

Here are a couple more thoughts for you regarding getting approval and selecting your team.  You may be considered the recruitment representative on the team, but you may also have HR or field expertise to bring to the table.  If you serve both roles, don’t overextend your budget or make the team too large by including more people than necessary. 

Also, when planning the budget and paying for the event, don’t forget to include travel expenses and arrangements.
