Merit Promotion and Reinstatement

Merit Promotion announcements are open to candidates who are current or former Federal employees with career or career-conditional status.  Candidates eligible to apply under these announcements are referred to as “status candidates.”

Reinstatement applies to candidates who have held a career or career-conditional appointment in the past.  To be reinstated, an employee who held a career-conditional appointment must apply for reinstatement within three years of the separation.  However, there is no time limit for candidates who achieved career status, or who are career-conditional and have veteran’s preference.
