Lesson Overview

When recruiting, you’ll need to provide potential applicants with as much information as possible about the position and the hiring process.

NRCS has several avenues through which it can hire employees, referred to as Federal hiring authorities.  NRCS actively recruits employees that can be hired under these various hiring authorities.  Some authorities are less used than others; however, you may find a person who qualifies for employment under any of these authorities and you should be able to point that person in the right direction whether you’re actively recruiting or not.

This lesson will give you an overview of available hiring authorities and programs through which NRCS may hire its employees.  The lessons that follow in this module will give you more information on each program individually.

This lesson will take approximately 15 minutes to complete.
