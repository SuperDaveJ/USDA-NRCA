STEP Guidelines

STEP employees are students working on a temporary basis for NRCS.  These employees must be enrolled at least half-time in an accredited high school, technical or vocational school, or 2- or 4-year college or university.  A STEP’s NRCS assignments do not have to be tied to their educational or career path.  Therefore, students of any discipline can be hired as STEPs, allowing more flexibility for both students and managers.

Positions to be filled by STEPs do not need to be posted—STEPs can be hired directly.  However, an applicant will be required to submit a resume and proof of enrollment in a qualifying educational institution.  A STEP’s pay will be based on their level of education, and while they do not receive benefits, they will earn annual and sick leave.  STEPs cannot be converted into full-time employees; however, they can be converted into SCEPs, which can be converted into a full-time employee once they have met the necessary requirements.
