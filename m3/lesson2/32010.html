Lesson Overview

Student and intern programs are a major source of employees for NRCS.  As a recruiter, a large part of your audience will be looking to participate in these programs.  For these programs, candidates will need to be enrolled in a high school, college or university, or vocational or technical school.  This is one reason that establishing relationships with schools is so vital.

This lesson will take approximately 20 minutes to complete.
