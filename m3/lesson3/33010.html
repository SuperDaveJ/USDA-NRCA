Lesson Overview

The remaining authorities through which NRCS typically hires employees can be divided into three categories:

  • Veterans
  • Schedule A
  • Delegated Examining Unit (DEU)

So you’ll be prepared to answer potential applicants’ questions about these hiring authorities, this lesson will introduce you to the different authorities under these categories, who qualifies for them, and how they can be used. 

This lesson will take approximately 20 minutes to complete.
