Veteran Hiring Authorities

Several special hiring authorities exist for U.S. veterans.  These authorities are designed as a benefit to citizens who have previously served in the Armed Forces to give them special appointing consideration when applying for Federal positions. 

Specifically, there are three authorities: 

  • Veterans’ Recruitment Appointment (VRA) Authority
  • Veterans Employment Opportunities Act (VEOA) Authority
  • 30% or More Disabled Veteran authority
