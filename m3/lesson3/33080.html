Schedule A Hiring

Audio Transcript

The Schedule A hiring authority allows individuals with severe physical disabilities, psychiatric disabilities, or mental retardation to apply for non-competitive appointment.Â  These applicants must provide the following documentation of their eligibility under Schedule A:

  • Proof of disability from licensed medical professionals, state or private vocational rehabilitation specialists, or any Government agency that issues or provides disability benefits, and
  • Certification from the same individuals that provided the proof of disability stating that the applicant is likely to succeed in the performance of the duties of the position for which he or she is applying.
