Expert Recruiter

I bet you’re thinking, “Wow, there’s a lot I need to know about the rules for veterans’ hiring authorities, especially if this is just the first of three!” 
There are a lot of guidelines associated with veterans’ hiring authorities, but keep in mind that this training is not designed to make you an expert.  These overviews are to give you a general idea of what’s out there so you can talk intelligently to your potential applicants.

You can come back to this training to refresh yourself before you go to an event, and there are links to more information in the Resources section of the course.  Also, the hiring officials that go with you to recruiting events are a great source for answering complex questions about this topic.  Don’t be afraid to refer people who need details about these authorities to HR or to the OPM website!
