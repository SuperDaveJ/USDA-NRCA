Delegated Examining Unit (DEU)

If an applicant does not qualify under any of the hiring authorities you’ve learned about so far in this module, he or she may still be considered for employment through the process of the DEU.  The DEU is the result of an authority that allows the Federal government to recruit and hire employees from the general public.

These vacancies must be posted on USAJobs.gov, and the area of consideration on the announcement will be listed at “US Citizens.”  No previous Federal service is required to be considered for DEU positions.
