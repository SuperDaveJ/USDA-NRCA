30% or More Disabled Veteran

The 30% or More Disabled Veteran authority allows any veteran with a 30% or more service-connected disability to be non-competitively appointed.

Eligible applicants:

  • Are retired from active military service with a service-connected disability rating of 30% or more; OR
  • Have a rating by the Department of Veterans Affairs showing a compensable service-connected disability of 30% or more.

This authority can be used to make permanent, temporary (not to exceed 1 year) or term (more than 1 year, but not more than 4) appointments in the competitive service. There is no grade level restriction.

For permanent positions, applicants are first placed on a time-limited appointment of at least 60 days and are converted to a permanent appointment at management's discretion.
